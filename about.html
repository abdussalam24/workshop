AW – Automotive Repair & Maintenance Service Specialist

Auto Workshop is a general automobile repair workshop with an excellent reputation for providing best quality services. We are based in Karachi, Pakistan and operated with over 38 years of experience in automotive industry. Whether you need computer diagnostics or routine car maintenance, we have you covered.

AW is your final destination for all kind of automotive repair services. We have certified mechanics in our Auto workshop, who ensure your vehicle continuously receives a best auto repair service.

AW wants you to come to us because we save you time and cash by obtaining your repairs right the first time. You will keep coming to AW because you can trust our expertise and our best automotive counseling.

Our customers relish having a team in waiting with in-depth automotive information, personalized care, and honest evaluation.

We want to make Auto Workshop as your first auto repair shop. We build lasting relationships with all of our customers and staff through honesty, integrity, and goodwill.

Having a similar equipment that the dealers do, we’re able to service numerous complicated parts of your automobile. We use the best quality parts once servicing your vehicle, guaranteeing its long life and reliability.
